About Us

At Siya Enterprises, we understand that efficient, high-quality printing is crucial for the success of your business. That's why we are committed to providing you with the best commercial printers and office automation systems on the market.



As a trusted channel partner for eight leading multinational companies in the office automation industry, we bring a wealth of expertise and a proven track record to our clients. With over 13 years of experience, we have successfully served 750 clients and completed more than 1,000 installations. Our commitment to excellence ensures that our clients receive top-tier products and services tailored to meet their specific business needs. Choose us for reliable, efficient, and innovative office automation solutions that drive productivity and success.

Whether you need reliable machines for high-volume printing, multifunctional devices to streamline your office tasks, or advanced solutions for automated workflows, we have the right products to meet your needs.

  • Expert Service: Our team of experienced professionals provides exceptional customer service and technical support, ensuring your equipment operates at peak performance.
  • Customized Solutions: We tailor our solutions to fit your specific business requirements, helping you increase productivity and reduce costs.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any other questions, please feel free to reach out to our customer service team. We are here to help!

1. What types of printers do you offer?

We offer a wide range of commercial printers including laser printers, inkjet printers, multifunction printers, and high-volume production printers from leading brands.

2. What brands of printers do you carry?

We carry top brands such as Kodak Alaris, Canon, evolis, Konika Minolta, Epson, Riso, TOA and Ricoh, ensuring high-quality and reliable performance for your business needs.

3. Can you help with setting up and installing new printers?

Absolutely! Our team of experts will assist you with the setup and installation of your new printers to ensure they are ready to use.

4. Do you offer maintenance and repair services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance and repair services to keep your printers running smoothly. Our technicians are certified and experienced in handling all types of printer issues.

5. How can I schedule a service appointment?

You can schedule a service appointment by contacting us through our phone number, email, or the contact form on our website. We will arrange a convenient time for our technicians to visit your location.

6. Do you provide supplies like toner and ink cartridges?

Yes, we supply a wide range of toner, ink cartridges, and other consumables for various printer models. You can order these supplies directly from our website or by contacting our sales team.

7. Can you help with office automation solutions?

Yes, we offer a variety of office automation systems to streamline your workflows, including document management systems, automated mailing solutions, and more.

8. How can I get a quote for your products and services?

To get a quote, please contact our sales team via phone or email, or fill out the request form on our website. We will provide you with a detailed quotation based on your specific needs.

9. Do you offer training on how to use the new printers and automation systems?

Yes, we provide training sessions for your staff to ensure they are fully proficient in using the new equipment and systems. This can be arranged at the time of installation or as needed.

10. Can you assist with upgrading our current office equipment?

Yes, we can evaluate your existing equipment and recommend upgrades to improve efficiency and productivity. Contact us to schedule a consultation.
